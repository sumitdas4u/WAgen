Got Questions?

What is WagenAI and how does it work?+
WagenAI is an AI-powered WhatsApp bot and website chatbot for growing businesses. Train it on your website, PDFs, or FAQs - it replies to customer questions 24/7 and captures leads automatically.
WhatsApp QR vs WhatsApp Business API — which should I use?+
WhatsApp QR — scan with your existing WhatsApp Business number, live in 2 minutes, no approval needed. Perfect to get started immediately.

WhatsApp Business API — official Meta-verified integration for scale, verified green tick, unlimited conversations. Available on Growth and Pro plans.
What counts as one conversation?+
One conversation = all messages with a single customer within a 24-hour window. A customer sending 10 messages in one day = 1 conversation. Simple, fair counting — no per-message billing.
Are integrations like CRM and Google Sheets available?+
Google Sheets integration and API/webhook automations are available now. All captured leads are stored in your WagenAI conversation dashboard, and additional native CRM connectors will continue to expand.
Does WagenAI support multiple languages?+
Yes. WagenAI can automatically detect and respond in multiple major languages. No extra setup is needed.
Is there a free trial?+
Yes — free trial with no credit card required. Sign up, train your AI, and go live on WhatsApp or your website in under 5 minutes.
