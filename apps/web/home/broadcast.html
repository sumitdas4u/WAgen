FAQ

Common questions

Can I send broadcast messages to any phone number? +
You can only message contacts who have opted in via your WhatsApp Business number — either through a QR code, website widget, or manual import. Meta requires opt-in consent for all template messages.
What is a WhatsApp template and do I need approval? +
Yes. All broadcast messages must use pre-approved WhatsApp templates created in Meta Business Manager. WAgen syncs your approved templates automatically. Approval typically takes a few hours.
Is there a limit on how many contacts I can broadcast to? +
No hard limit per campaign, but your Meta account has a daily messaging tier (1K, 10K, 100K contacts/day) based on your Business Manager quality rating. WAgen distributes sending across the day to stay within limits.
How does retargeting work? +
After a campaign is sent, WAgen tracks delivery and read status per contact. You can then create a retarget campaign that automatically sends a different template to contacts who didn't read the original — with one click.
