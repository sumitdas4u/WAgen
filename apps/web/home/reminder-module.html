How it works

Two parts. Set up once. Runs forever.

The Reminder Module has two engines working together — a capture flow that collects dates and a campaign engine that fires messages automatically.

①

Contact is added or updated

When a customer is created or their profile is updated, WAgen checks your conditions — contact type, tags, missing birthday field — and sends a permission template.

②

Customer taps YES

One tap on the quick-reply button triggers your linked WhatsApp Flow. The flow asks for the date, validates it, and saves it directly to the contact's field.

③

Campaign fires automatically

Every day WAgen scans for contacts whose date matches today (or N days from today). It sends each campaign step — 15-day teaser, 3-day nudge, day-of message.

Two dispatch modes.
Annual or one-time.

Birthday and anniversary reminders should fire every year — WAgen matches the day and month, ignores the year, and deduplicates per calendar year. Event dates like policy renewals should fire once on the exact stored date.

  • Annual: matches month + day every year — perfect for birthdays, anniversaries
  • Exact date: matches the full stored date — fires once, then stops
  • Duplicate guard built in — same contact never gets the same step twice
  • Preview upcoming dispatches before they go out

FAQ

Common questions

Does the customer have to fill a form to share their birthday? +
No forms. WAgen sends a WhatsApp permission template — a pre-approved message with a quick-reply button. The customer taps "Yes", your linked WhatsApp Flow asks for the date in a conversational way, and it's saved automatically to their contact profile.
What if the customer doesn't respond to the permission template? +
The session expires after 24 hours. You can configure a retry interval (e.g. try again after 7 days) and a max retry count (e.g. up to 2 retries). After max retries are exhausted, the contact is not prompted again. If they decline ("Not now"), a cooldown period (default 30 days) prevents re-prompting.
Will it send the same birthday message twice in one year? +
No. Every dispatch is logged with the campaign year and step ID. The system uses a unique constraint — if the same contact has already received a given step in the same year, it's skipped. This applies to each step individually, so a 15-day teaser and a day-of message are tracked separately.
Can I send different offers for different customer segments? +
Yes. Each reminder config has its own audience filter conditions — identical to the Sequences filter engine. You can create a "Birthday — VIP" config with premium offers and a separate "Birthday — Regular" config with standard offers, each targeting different tags or contact types.
What is the difference between Annual and Exact Date dispatch? +
Annual mode ignores the year — it matches only the month and day. A birthday stored as 15 March 1990 will fire on 15 March every year. Exact Date matches the full date including year — a policy renewal stored as 20 June 2026 fires once on 20 June 2026 (or N days before) and never again.
Does this use WhatsApp templates or free-text messages? +
Only approved WhatsApp templates are used — both for the permission ask and each campaign step. This is required for all messages sent outside the 24-hour customer service window. You create and approve templates in Meta Business Manager, then link them to your reminder steps in WAgen.
