Common Questions

Pricing Questions Answered

Is there a free trial?+
Yes — WagenAI offers a free trial with no credit card required. You can set up your WhatsApp AI bot and website widget and go live in under 5 minutes.
What counts as one conversation?+
One conversation = a 24-hour window of messages with a single customer — whether on WhatsApp or your website chat. If the same customer messages again after 24 hours, it starts a new conversation. Multiple back-and-forth messages within the same 24-hour window count as just one conversation.
What happens if I exceed my monthly conversations?+
Extra conversations are charged at ₹5 per conversation beyond your plan limit. You'll never get cut off — your bot continues to work and the overage is added to your next bill.
Can I cancel anytime?+
Yes. WagenAI is a month-to-month subscription. You can cancel anytime from your dashboard — no questions asked, no cancellation fees.
Are CRM and Google Sheets integrations available?+
These integrations are coming soon. Currently, all captured leads are stored in your WagenAI dashboard. CRM sync and Google Sheet export will roll out on Growth and Pro plans.
Is WagenAI cheaper than WATI and AiSensy?+
Yes. WagenAI Starter is ₹799/month vs WATI at ₹2,499 and AiSensy at ₹1,500. WagenAI also includes true AI (not rule-based), instant QR start, website widget, and document upload — features competitors charge extra for or don't offer at all.
