Copy your WagenAI embed script from the dashboard and paste it before the closing </body> tag. Works on WordPress, Shopify, Wix, Webflow, or any custom HTML site. Live in 60 seconds.

  src="https://cdn.wagenai.com/widget.js"
  data-wid="YOUR_WIDGET_ID"
  data-color="#1DB954"

></script>

FAQ

Website Widget Questions Answered

How do I add the WagenAI widget to my website?+
Copy your WagenAI embed script from your dashboard and paste it before the closing </body> tag on your site. Works on WordPress, Shopify, Wix, Webflow, and any custom HTML site. Takes under 60 seconds.
Does the widget automatically learn from my website?+
Yes. Paste your website URL in WagenAI and it will crawl your pages and build a knowledge base from your content. Every time your website updates, you can re-train with one click.
Can I customise the widget's colour and appearance?+
Yes. You can customise the chat bubble colour, greeting message, bot name, avatar, and widget position (bottom-left or bottom-right) to match your brand identity.
Does the website widget capture leads automatically?+
Yes. WagenAI naturally collects visitor name and phone number during conversation when intent is shown. All leads are stored in your dashboard with full chat history.
Is the website widget included in all plans?+
Yes. The website chat widget is included in all WagenAI plans including the free trial — no extra charge.
