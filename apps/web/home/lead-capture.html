FAQ

Lead Capture Questions Answered

How does WagenAI capture leads automatically?+
WagenAI detects buying intent during a conversation and naturally asks the customer for their name and phone number — just like a real salesperson would. No forms, no cold pop-ups. The lead is saved instantly to your dashboard.
What information does WagenAI capture from each lead?+
WagenAI captures the customer's name, phone number, and specific requirement or service interest — plus the source channel (WhatsApp or website), date, and full conversation history.
Does lead capture work on both WhatsApp and website?+
Yes. WagenAI captures leads from both your WhatsApp Business number (QR Mode or API) and your website chat widget. All leads appear in one unified dashboard.
Can I export leads to Google Sheets or a CRM?+
Google Sheets integration and API workflows are available now. All leads are still stored in your WagenAI dashboard where you can view, search, and manage them.
Is there a limit on how many leads I can capture?+
Lead capture is available on all plans. The number of conversations per month varies by plan (Starter: 300, Growth: 600, Pro: 1,200), but every conversation can result in a captured lead.
