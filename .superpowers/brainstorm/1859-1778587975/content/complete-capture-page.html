⚡ How This Works
📄
1. Template Sent When a matching contact is created/updated, WAgen sends the permission template with their name pre-filled.
👆
2. Customer Taps YES The quick-reply button triggers the linked flow automatically.
🤖
3. Flow Captures Date Your flow asks for the date and saves it to the birthday contact field.
🎂
4. Campaign Fires On the birthday date, the campaign scheduler sends the reminder template automatically.
⚠️ Flow must save to the birthday field Make sure your selected flow has a "Save to Contact Field" node mapped to birthday. Otherwise the campaign will have no data to work from.
